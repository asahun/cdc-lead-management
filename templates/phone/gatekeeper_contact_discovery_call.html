Call Script – Gatekeeper / Contact Discovery

Purpose: identify the correct internal contact (CFO, Controller, AP, Treasury, Compliance) for an unclaimed-property item under [OwnerName].

1. Opening

You:

Hi, this is [ProfileFirstName] with LoadRouter here in Georgia. I’m hoping you can help me find the appropriate contact regarding an unclaimed-property item reported under [OwnerName].

(Pause and let them respond.)

2. Credibility Statement

You:

This isn’t a sales call. The State of Georgia is holding an unclaimed-property item reported under your company’s name, and I just need to make sure it reaches the correct financial contact internally.

3. Ask for Routing

You:

Could you point me to whoever handles AP compliance, treasury, or unclaimed-property matters?

4. If They Ask What This Is About

You:

Sure — the state publishes these records publicly. One of the items was reported under your company, and I just want to route it properly so it can be verified.

5. If They Ask for Your Info

You:

Name: [ProfileFullName]

Company: LoadRouter, LLC

Purpose: Unclaimed-property record match for [OwnerName]

Email: [ProfileEmail]

Phone: [ProfilePhone]

6. If They Hesitate

You:

Totally understand — nothing needs to be decided now. I just want to send the details to the right person so they can verify it on the state portal.

7. If They Still Seem Unsure

You:

Would this typically go to your Controller, CFO, Treasury, or AP Compliance team?

8. If They Provide a Contact

You:

Perfect, thank you so much — I’ll send the summary for their review. I appreciate your help.
