Avoiding Scams and Misleading Notices

Unclaimed property notices are sometimes imitated by fraudulent solicitations. A legitimate communication will allow you to:

  • Verify the property through the official state website
  • Confirm the sender’s business registration
  • Independently validate contact information
  • Decline representation without obligation
  • Review all documents prior to signing

We encourage you to validate any solicitation — including this one — using the State of Georgia’s public search portal and by confirming Load Router, LLC in the Georgia Secretary of State business registry.
